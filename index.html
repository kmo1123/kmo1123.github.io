1. 2022 - 2023 H5N1 Bird Flu Modeling and Prediction in the United States

Read more about this project (open in new window)

Jan. 2023 - Mar. 2023

  • This is a project for the International Association of Statistical Computing (IASC) Data Analysis Competition 2023.
  • Worked with two students at UC Davis, Weilin Cheng and Henyuan Liu, and two students at UMich, Sida Tian and Li Yuan.
  • Built and compared statistical models which predicts which county in the upcoming month will have H5N1 case(s).
  • The prediction accuracy of the model is 98.4% with AUC 0.8015.
  • Provided some suggestions including collecting more related data such as egg production and breeding size to improve the model.
  • Proposed some advice on how to control the spread of H5N1 virus.
  • The team won 2nd place and a travel grant (600 EUR) for attending the World Statistics Conference (WSC) 2023 Conference organized by ISI.
